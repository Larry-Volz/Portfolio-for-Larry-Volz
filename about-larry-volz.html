About Larry Volz

Hi everyone, I'm Larry. Sometimes people call me "Doc".

Before going into software engineering full-time, web development and systems programming had been a sideline of mine for decades.

As an escape room owner I have created embedded systems from scratch (C/C++/Python/ Arduino/Raspberry Pi) to be hidden in devious puzzles designed to challenge and entertain my players. (moo ha haaaa!!)

I've built dozens of websites over the years for small businesses (HTML/CSS/WordPress) including a full-stack CRM for a national therapist referral company (JavaScript/PHP/SQL).

I built the website you're looking at now using mostly jQuery and Bootstrap.

A few years ago I decided to turn my love of software development into a full-time profession and went back to school. At this writing I am two classes away from an associates in computer science (I have a bachelor's degree in physical science as well). I have taken college coursework in Java, Python, operating systems, software engineering, networking & communications, SQL and web programming.

Recently I was also accepted into Springboard's competitive full-stack software engineering bootcamp where I am working very hard at a great curriculum that has been honing my skills in advanced Javascript, Git/GitHub, unit testing and RESTful API's (my favorite!).

By the time I am done I will be a certified software engineer with solid front and back-end skills including Node, ReactJS, Redux, Flask, postgresQL, SQLalchemy and express,

I am VERY excited about joining a creative team to work together to make beautiful and effective code.

Creating is one of the things I enjoy most in life. You might have picked up on that from all the crazy pictures of me.

I spent my younger years traveling the world as an illusionist and comedy hypnotist. And then later I have spent a lot of time helping people challenge and change bad habits through hypnotherapy.

So I like to think that I'm fun to be around, a VERY dedicated and self-starting project manager and have great communication and motivational soft skills.

If you are an employer or contractor looking for a new consultant or developer -- I hope that you'll find my skillset and portfolio to your liking. I'd love to talk with you.
